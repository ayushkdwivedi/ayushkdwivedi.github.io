I am a research scholar (Ph.D.) at Signal Processing and Communication research Centre (SPCRC), International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Hyderabad. I am working under the guidance of Dr. Sachin Chaudhari. I am an Electronics and Communication Engineering (E.C.E) graduate currently working in the field of wireless communication.

My area of interest for research is next generation wireless communication. At present I am reading hybrid satellite-terrestial communication complementing 5G network. Previously I have done under graduation project on basic antenna designs. I have also designed music recommender system using machine learning as part of coursework in SMAI course at IIIT. I am interested in exploring possibilies of using machine learning in next generation wireless communication.

Apart from being a researcher, I enjoy most of my time in literature, poetry, public speaking and gardening. I like reading books. I am greatly influenced by Ramcharitmanas, a masterpiece by Goswami Tulsidas. I am an award winnig public speaker at multiple stages. Maintaining my own grown plants is one of my favoutite pastime.
